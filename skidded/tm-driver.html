DESCRIPTION

Browser game TM Driver allows players to drive around the streets of the virtual city and perform various missions.

This is a high quality game that you can play absolutely for free. It is supported by all web browsers. Here you will drive a car at high speed and improve your racing skills.

The gameplay takes place on a large map, where difficult missions are waiting for you. You need to successfully cope with each of them. You will receive great gifts for each successfully completed mission. Opportunities to get new cars directly depend on the successful completion of each mission set before you in the game.

Cars of different capacities and colors will be available to you in the process of passing the game. Get the real pleasure of driving an off-road car. But remember that you are closely watched by the members of the team of riders who have to make a decision about whether they will accept you into their team.

You can perform different stunts and try your off-road driving skills. Many exciting and breathtaking adventures are waiting for you on the way to victory. Go through all the difficulties with dignity and in the end you will get the title of winner.

You can control your car using the keyboard.
