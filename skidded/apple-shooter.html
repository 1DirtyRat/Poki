DESCRIPTION

Apple Shooter is a fun archery game where you have to shoot an apple off the head of a friend. This online shooting game was clearly inspired by the legendary archery champ Wilhelm Tell and developed by Wolf Games. To become a marksman takes strength, poise, power and grace. As Apple Shooter will show, it also takes an endless list of volunteers willing to balance an apple on top of their head. Because if you should miss, there's a reasonable chance your arrow will cause some awfully fatal damage.

Each level will move your target back a little making the next shot ever so slightly more challenging. Adjust the tension of your bowstring, aim at the right height and hope for the best. When you are in the more challenging levels, you should be very careful and work yourself ahead in small steps.
