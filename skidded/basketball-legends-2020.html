DESCRIPTION

The basketball championship is back! Play as your favorite NBA team in the new Basketball Legends 2020 Unblocked game. You can play a training match, a random match or play in a tournament mode and win your Larry O'Brien NBA Championship Trophy. All NBA stars in one place - LeBron James, James Harden, Kawhi Leonard, Giannis Antetokounmpo, Luka Doncic and many more. Also, if you play at school, then you can play with your friend. Find out which one of you is cooler in Basketball Legends 2020!

Controls

Player One:

  • WASD - Move
  • B - Shoot / Perform action
  • S - Pump / Block
  • V - Supershot
  • D (twice) - Dash

Player Two:

  • Arrow keys - Move
  • L - Shoot / Perform action
  • DOWN Arrow - Pump / Block
  • K - Supershot
  • LEFT Arrow (twice) - Dash
