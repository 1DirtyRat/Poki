DESCRIPTION

Sports heads basketball is an exciting game about playing basketball. You can play the unblocked game at school and even with your parents.

Here you can choose a single basketball player for yourself and go to the sports ground where your opponent has been waiting for you. The fight will take place one on one, and the one who scores more goals in a certain time will win. To succeed the player must move very fast and make quick decisions. There is some bonus that gives you benefits or harms you. Moreover, you are able to enjoy this game with your friend. It is awesome!

The singularity of the game is that the body of footballer consists of only one head and with it, you have to play basketball. I honestly recommend you to play it!
