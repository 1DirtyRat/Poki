DESCRIPTION

Monkey Mart is a management game that puts you in charge of a charming monkey character, who is tasked with running a bustling supermarket. From planting fruits to harvesting produce and filling up the stands with various food items, you'll have your hands full as you sell bananas, corn, eggs, peanuts, coffee beans, chocolate, wheat, and much more. Keep an eye out for your customers who will visit the cashier desk once they have picked up their desired products. You simply need to stand next to the cash register to collect your well-deserved earnings.

As you progress through the game and unlock new aisles, you can hire assistants to help maintain the aisles and other employees. With more products, you can also buy appliances that can prepare advanced items such as chocolate bars, coffee, yogurt, popcorn, peanut butter, muffins, cookies, ice cream, and more. To be the best manager in town, you'll need to upgrade your character's managing skills, unlock new work stations, and teach your staff new skills. Don't forget to wear cool hats to manage your market in style.

Do you have what it takes to make your farmer's market the best one in town? Play Monkey Mart now and find out!
