DESCRIPTION

If you like not just to play games, but also to solve various puzzles, then you will like the Bloxorz unblocked game. During it, you need to move the rectangle on the playing field. As a result, it should fall into the hole. It seems very simple, doesn't it? But no, because you can stumble upon a variety of cunning traps that you will able to get around with the help of it.

The main objective of the game is to establish a rectangle on a special square of the playing field, which is highlighted in a special way, with a minimum number of moves. Each new stage of the game involves increasing the level of difficulty and a new puzzle.
