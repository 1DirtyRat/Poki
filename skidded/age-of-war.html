DESCRIPTION

Age of War is an unblocked game where your purpose is to destroy the rival’s base. You can play this game only alone but it is free. As it named Age of War – it has 5 ages, respectively. Starting with the Stone Age you will move to the future. To move to the next age all you need is to gain Xp – experience points. These points are credited in two cases: when you destroy rivals units and when one of your units is killed.

One interesting thing about this flash game is that you will be able to use a special attack, but it needs time to be filled before your next usage. There will be different special attacks on each age. The aim is to protect your base and to crash the enemy’s headquarters.
