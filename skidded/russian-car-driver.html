DESCRIPTION

Russian Car Driver Unblocked is a driving simulator. Before you start the game you can choose the colour for your car, as well as adjust other game settings for your convenience.

The game has several levels, and the complexity of their passage will increase in each subsequent level. The game is also available free level, you can use it to get a general idea of the principles of the game.

You will also have the opportunity to test your car driving capabilities in different weather conditions. Do not forget to follow the rules of the road, otherwise, you will chase the police. You can participate in a single or in a competitive race.
