DESCRIPTION

The Paint.io game is an unblocked browser game for any PC based on Chrome OS, Mac or Windows.

Paint.io is aimed at all people without specific age restrictions, so even a child can play it. Now kids won't interfere adults in their free time.

In the main menu, you can choose the character that you will play for. But some characters you will have to earn during the game. For example, to occupy more than 50% of the field or for killing a certain number of opponents.

The meaning of the game is to become a winner among the opponents. Your time is absolutely unlimited. Fill as much space as possible on the field. The game ends at the moment when someone touches you, when you are outside of your field limits. At the same time, there are many other players on the field who will try to kill you in order to take a leading position, you can do the same. Therefore, it is important to hook the enemy quickly while he is near you.

However, be careful not to touch the sides of the field, because in this case, the game will be finished for you. When playing the game, you reach a certain level and monitor the progress. You can control both a computer mouse and a keyboard, depending on what is more convenient for you.
