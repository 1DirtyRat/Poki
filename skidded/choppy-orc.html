Choppy Orc is a fast-paced action game that is sure to keep you on the edge of your seat. As the name suggests, you take on the role of a mighty orc warrior, charging through levels and slaying anything in your path. With its unique art style and addictive gameplay, Choppy Orc is a game that you won't want to put down.

The controls for Choppy Orc are simple and intuitive. Using the arrow keys, you move your orc warrior back and forth, avoiding obstacles and enemies as you go. Your orc will automatically attack enemies as he comes into contact with them, making the game feel fast and frenzied.

As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter different types of enemies, each with their own strengths and weaknesses. Some enemies are armored and will require multiple hits to take down, while others move more quickly and can be more difficult to avoid. Luckily, your orc can also pick up power-ups that will help him on his journey. These power-ups include health boosts, increased speed, and temporary invincibility.

One of the unique features of Choppy Orc is its "rage" mode. As you defeat enemies, you'll fill up a rage meter at the bottom of the screen. Once the meter is full, you can activate rage mode, which will make your orc faster and stronger for a short period of time. This is especially useful when you're facing tough enemies or need to make it through a difficult section of a level.

Choppy Orc is a thrilling and addictive game that is sure to keep you entertained for hours. With its simple controls, unique art style, and challenging gameplay, it's a great choice for anyone who loves action-packed games.
