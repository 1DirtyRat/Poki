DESCRIPTION

Pixel Gun Apocalypse Unblocked is a Minecraft-style multiplayer first-person shooter in which all characters and maps are made up of blocks. There are 2 modes available for the game - Deathmatch and Team Deathmatch. In Deathmatch mode, you fight other opponents, which can be up to 16 people. Your task is to stay alive. In Team Deathmatch mode, you are divided into 2 teams: one team has people, the other has zombies. You start playing for people and if you die, then go to the team of zombies and start hunting for survivors. A pistol and a machine gun are available from the weapon. Good luck!
