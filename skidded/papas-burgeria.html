DESCRIPTION

There are a huge number of chain establishments in the world that prepare and sell burgers. There are so many of them that it would be difficult to count, but among true connoisseurs of burgers, such places are not valued. Such people prefer small establishments with custom burgers prepared by real lovers of their craft. There is a game that is about this. It's called Papa's Burgeria.

You can choose one character from three and open your own burger shop. At Papa's Burgeria, you need to take an order and then prepare a burger according to the wishes of the client. All clients have different preferences and it is necessary to listen to everyone in order to understand what needs to be done.

The main feature of this game is the variability in cooking burgers. Even though you will know the preferences of the customers, you can still add a little personality to each burger. You can add your "secret ingredient", change the sauce, or completely change the composition of the burger. It all depends on your imagination, but if you still want to earn as much money as possible, then you should still listen to the wishes of your customers.

At Papa's Burgeria, you can immerse yourself in the world of cooking and feel like a really cool chef who gives everything he has to make a delicious burger.
