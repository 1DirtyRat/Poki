DESCRIPTION

Are you ready for a crazy Christmas adventure at Subway Surfers London? The capital of Britain at this time of the year is covered with snow. Move through snow-covered locations in order not to fall into the hands of a policeman dressed as Santa Claus.

This runner gives you the opportunity not only to run but also to collect various bonuses during the round. Some of these bonuses are needed to multiply your earnings. During the race, you collect coins, which can then be spent in several sections of the Subway Surfers London menu. Firstly, you can spend money on customizing your character, you can buy him a new suit (skin) or a new surfboard (the game has the ability to stop running at any time and start swimming through the air like waves, but every broken board will cost you money). Secondly, you can purchase various boosts for equipment that you will use during the race.

Complete various tasks that are recorded in your diary and get rewards for this. The tasks may not be difficult, but the reward will make you glad for what you spent on this time (basically, as a reward there will be revival keys that will make it much easier for you to complete Subway Surfers London). Enjoy every obstacle you overcome and have fun.
