DESCRIPTION

The racing game where you will have to carry a special good. This is an egg. So, it means that all the actions must be done very carefully. If you drop the egg, it will be broken immediately. The main problem that the egg is oval, and the car can move too fast.

You need to control all the actions rather carefully. Though, the actions of moving are being done only with two buttons, the task is not so simple. You can use either arrows or the letters A and D. Do the accelerating and breaking smoothly in order not to drop the egg. In this case, you will have to start the level from the very beginning.

In Eggy Car, there are five cars available. You can buy them in the store inside the game. During your races, you will get coins. These coins can be spent on the purchase of the car. The idea is simple. The longer distance you cover, the more coins you will get.

The task is complicated because the road is not straight. There are hills and big ditches on the road. The gamer will have to be rather careful if he wants to be the egg up to the finish. Each car has its own advantages and disadvantages. When you have enough money, you can buy the car and test it. Probably, you will have to return to the previous variant. Eggy Car is done for the real lovers of extreme racing.
