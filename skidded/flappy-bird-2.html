DESCRIPTION

This new upgraded version of a famous unblocked game will definitely grab your free time! You are going to meet the familiar bird flying in new conditions.

As in the previous edition, the trajectory of the flight passes between high and lower pipes. But the skyline is now constantly rotating, so you should double your patience and triple you concentration to avoid a sudden collision.

The control is quite simple. With the left mouse click, you set the flying personage in motion. Try not to make mistakes and don’t let the brave bird fall, and your attempts will be awarded generously.
