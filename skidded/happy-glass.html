DESCRIPTION

The unlocked game Happy Glass is designed for fans of arcade games and puzzles. You'll have to apply your creative abilities to fill a sad glass with water to make it smile once again.

You will have to do your best to draw a path for water, leading straight into the glass. But be careful: the water should not pour out of the glass if you do not want to pass the stage again. The game will become harder during the passage. The game features 100 different levels. The gameplay of the game is convenient and simple.

You can play it not only in the browser but also on a smartphone with Android and IOS operating systems.
