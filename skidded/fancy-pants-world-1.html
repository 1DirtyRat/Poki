DESCRIPTION

Take control of our funky stickman and choose his pant color. Once you have geared up, step into the start door and through into the first level! The music is enchanting, the graphics are fun and inspiring, and the gameplay is fun and challenging.

Jump over platforms, squash spiders and gun weilding baddies and look out for the cardboard boxes that transport you to bonus levels! You must have quick reactions and time your jumps perfectly to reach all the areas of a level. Look out for golden keys and collect as many spirals as possible. Can you conquer every challenge and guide Fancy Pants through this magical world?
