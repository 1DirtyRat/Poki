DESCRIPTION

In the unblocked game Papa Louie you will become a sophisticated chef who fights with pizza monsters. Earn coins and travel through a strange surreal world where food hunts for you.

Pizza monsters take visitors to the restaurant and colleagues of the chef as hostages; you have to save everyone. In the game you will try to defeat scary monsters who spit cheese to get pizza and take it to your restaurant. Along the way you will collect coins that you can spend on buying pepper bombs, which are very effective against predatory vegetables. Find out who is behind the mysterious appearance of terrible monsters!
