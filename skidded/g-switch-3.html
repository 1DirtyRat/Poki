DESCRIPTION

In G Switch 3 Unblocked you will play as a robot with one interesting ability – to switch gravitation when he wants. There are three buttons to play – endless, multiplayer and play. In multiplayer, you can choose from different robots, it can be a green and oval or yellow box-head robot or etc. “Play” mode truly has fewer obstacles than “endless”, but second is interesting in its way.

You can play with friends in “multiplayer” mode or train logic and reaction in casual mode. This game force you to sit down for a long time on your chair thereby helps to develop and to improve your skills.
